# BUSINESS Epic - OPEN QUESTIONS: Business Strategy Choices

The following strategic questions require resolution before commercial launch:

---

## 1. Marketplace Fee Split
- **Question:** What percentage should K.A.O.S charge on plugin and MCP tool sales in the marketplace?
- **Options:**
  - **Standard 30%:** Matches Apple/Google. High margin but could turn off developer communities.
  - **Developer-Friendly 10%:** Attracts more creators, counting on high volume.
  - **Flat monthly fee for developers:** Charging plugin developers a flat SDK fee, keeping 100% of sales.

---

## 2. Bootstrapping vs. Funding
- **Question:** Should the project raise venture capital to accelerate the Windows OS native launcher, or scale slowly through organic developer adoption?
- **Discussion:** Raising VC would speed up distribution but pressure the platform to centralize data or implement cloud monetization, threatening the "privacy first" core principle.
