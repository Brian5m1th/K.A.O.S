# BUSINESS Epic - RISKS: Commercial Risks

This document analyzes external and market risks to K.A.O.S's commercial success.

---

## 1. Competitive Risk: Native OS AI

### Risk
Windows Recall or Microsoft Copilot integrations could become deeply embedded in the operating system, providing native file watchers, process supervisors, and memory logs.

### Impact
- **High:** Reduces the value proposition of a third-party Cognitive OS.

### Mitigation
- Position K.A.O.S as **Platform-Agnostic** (runs identically on Windows, Linux, and macOS).
- Focus on **Developer Workflows** (KIRL code audit, local git history validation, and custom Wasm plugins) which Microsoft's consumer-focused OS AI doesn't prioritize.
- Highlight the **100% Privacy & Local-First** guarantee, avoiding any automatic telemetry to Microsoft's cloud.

---

## 2. Adoption Risk: Installation Friction

### Risk
If building the installer is delayed, users will continue to drop out during manual container setup.

### Impact
- **Critical:** Limits the product to advanced technical enthusiasts.

### Mitigation
- Prioritize Wave 4's bundler over advanced features. Ensure a working MSI exists before marketing sync subscriptions.
