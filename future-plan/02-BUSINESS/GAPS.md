# BUSINESS Epic - GAPS: Commercial Obstacles

The following gaps must be resolved to unlock K.A.O.S's market potential:

| Business Gap | Impact | Difficulty | Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **No One-Click Installer** | Stops non-dev adoption. Users must configure Docker/Python manually. | High | **Critical** |
| **No Stripe/Billing Portal** | Inability to collect payments for syncing or premium licenses. | Medium | High |
| **No Marketplace Registry**| No central repository index to publish or download plugins/MCPs. | High | Medium |
| **No Licensing Server** | Inability to issue and validate enterprise seats for KIRL audits. | Medium | High |
| **No Cloud Sync Bridge** | Users are locked to one local machine; no mobile companion. | High | Medium |
